What's in my monthly payment?

Your payment typically covers four things: principal (paying down the loan), interest (the lender's fee), property taxes, and homeowner's insurance. Lenders often call this "PITI."

How does interest rate affect cost?

Even a small rate difference adds up over 30 years. A half-percent lower rate on a $280k loan can save you over $30,000 in interest. It's worth shopping around with a few lenders.

Should I choose 15 or 30 years?

A 15-year loan has a higher monthly payment but you'll pay dramatically less interest overall. A 30-year gives you more breathing room month to month. Run both scenarios above to compare.
